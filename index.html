Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

Challenge your engineering mind with this mechanical puzzle game where you connect nuts and bolts to solve complex challenges.

About the Game

Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is an engaging mechanical puzzle game that challenges your problem-solving skills and engineering intuition. Each level presents unique challenges where you must connect different types of nuts and bolts to create working mechanisms.

Game Features

  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Various types of nuts and bolts
  • Physics-based mechanics
  • Intuitive controls
  • Progressive difficulty

How to Play

Use your mouse or touch screen to drag and connect different mechanical parts. Each level has specific objectives that you need to achieve by creating the correct mechanical connections.

Tips & Tricks

  • Plan your connections before making them
  • Pay attention to the size and type of nuts and bolts
  • Use the physics system to your advantage
  • Don't be afraid to experiment
  • Take your time to understand each level's mechanics
